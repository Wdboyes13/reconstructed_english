# Rules

1. `ph` in words like phone or phonology should become a single `f`.
2. Drop the letter `c`, replace all instances of `c` with a k or s.
3. Remove all silent letters, for example `carriage` should becomes `kárij`
4. Words should be spelt as close to their pronunciation as possible, for example coffee becomes `kɔfē`

## translation

1. `ph` in wûrds lík føn ør funɔlujē šud bēcum á singl `f`.
2. Drɔp þa letė `c`, rēplás al instinsis of `c` wiþ a k ør s.
3. Rēmōv al síliň letės, fôr exampl `carriage` šôd bēcums `kárij`
4. Wûrds šôd bē spelt as clós tō þár prónuncēášn as pɔsibl, fôr exampl `coffee` bēcums `kɔfē`
