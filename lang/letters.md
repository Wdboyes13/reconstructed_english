# Letters

- š - sh as in share
- í - ai as in buy
- á - ay/ae as in way
- û - o as in words
- þ - th as in thought
- ṅ - in as in pink
- ŋ̇ - ing as in baking
- ň - nt as in mint
- ē - ee as in bean
- ɔ - aw as in saw
- ə - ull in full
- ė - er as in were
- ō - oo as in moon
- ǒ - ou as in about
- ó - oe as in row
- ø - oa as in oar or shore
